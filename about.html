Paul — Lead Builder

Honest work, careful planning, and craftsmanship that lasts.

Paul has more than ten years of experience in general construction, home repairs, renovations, exterior projects, and custom carpentry. He takes pride in providing dependable work and straightforward communication from the first conversation through the finished project.

His experience covers projects of many sizes. That may mean repairing a door, rebuilding a deck, finishing custom trim, updating a kitchen, or organizing the many stages involved in constructing a complete new home.

Paul also focuses on new-home construction using Insulated Concrete Forms, commonly called ICF. These builds combine reinforced concrete and continuous insulation to create strong, efficient, and comfortable homes.

No matter the size of the job, his goal remains the same: understand what the customer needs, complete the work carefully, and leave behind a result they can feel confident about.
